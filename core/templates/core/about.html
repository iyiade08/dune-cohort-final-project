About CareConnect

We are on a mission to make
healthcare accessible to all

CareConnect was built to solve the real problem of accessing quality healthcare in Africa — long queues, no-shows, and poor record-keeping. We built a smarter way.

Our Story

Built for Africa's healthcare future

CareConnect started with a simple observation — booking a doctor's appointment in most African cities still involves phone calls, long waits, and paper records that get lost.


We built CareConnect to change that. A platform where patients can find verified doctors, book appointments in minutes, and manage their health history — all in one place.


For doctors, we provide tools to manage their schedules, consult with patients efficiently, and grow their practice. For clinic admins, we provide full oversight and reporting.
